About GlobeTrotter

A Platform For Asset Aggregation & Sale Of Billboards.

GlobeTrotterTM is a platform that aggregates over 25,000 billboards and out of home advertising assets of the 165 Outdoor Advertising Association of Nigeria members, Advertisers, media buyers and the General public into a very interactive digital marketplace.

  • Online access by Advertisers and Asset Owners

  • Uploading of billboard location, availability, and pricing by Asset owners

  • Finding billboards and making payments by Advertisers
